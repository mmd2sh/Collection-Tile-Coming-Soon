About Collection Brand

With over 27 years of experience in the Iranian tile and ceramic industry, Collection is a brand built on core values of responsibility, respect for customers, flexibility, and providing high-quality products. We pride ourselves on using the latest and most beautiful Italian designs, inspired by the unique patterns of natural stones.

At Collection, we offer a wide range of products to ensure that our customers can make the best choice with peace of mind. Our brand continually expands its market presence through new stores and a network of representatives in major cities, providing architects, project managers, construction companies, and consumers with quick and easy access to our products.

We are passionate about creating a good feeling in your home or workplace, because we believe that you deserve the best.
